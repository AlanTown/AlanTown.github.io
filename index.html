A Bit About Myself

At the time of making this website I am 18 years old and a computer science intended major. I'll be completely honest here, I am a huge geek when it comes to a number of things. A few examples include history, space and technology. There are actually pretty specific reasons why I'm so interested in this kinda stuff. There are two reasons why I love to learn about history. First I am a firm believer that history has patterns and that watching these patterns can serve as hints at what's to come. The second reason is simply that human history is the best story ever writen. I adore reading about anything from the Roman Legions to the world's dangerous first steps in to the modern world because you can't make some of these stories up. Now moving on to space. Put very simply I see space as the only possible solution to human growth and survival. Our fragile blue marble won't be around forever and we need to move on if our species is to survive. It also helps that I'm a huge science fiction nerd and would love to see some of the ideas in reality. Finaly lets look at tech. The ideas of that are starting to creep their ways into the hands of the public absolutely blow my mind. VR, Self-Driving Cars, and AI for example interest me to no end. Honestly part of the reason why I want to be a CS major is to be part of this kind of technology and it's development.
